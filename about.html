ABOUT SWIFT

SWIFT was originally created by Packie Lee in 2020, during the pandemic where people had to stay at home. As people could not go outside, he wanted to create a drink company that can be bought at home in order to convince people to exercise regularly even at home and hydrate themselves with this drink.

Process of SWIFT's Manufacture

Our SWIFT Company has many manufacturers and many factories in different countries, including USA, Vietnam, Thailand, China, etc. We produce different flavours of drinks and do purity checks before delivering our drinks to people.

Healthy contents

Our drink contains the healthiest contents for you. It has only contained 7% sugar, with 18% sodium, 10% zinc, and with different fruit flavours in our drinks.

Special discounts and offers

During your first purchase, using a promotion code that will be send to your email once you register, will get you 70% offer for your first drink purchased. We also do occasional event discounts such as the Christmas offer, the 11.11 sale, and many other event discounts and offers.
